APPS.

IGCSE MCQchecker

IGCSE MCQchecker is a web application designed to help students prepare for their IGCSE exams by providing a platform for practicing multiple-choice questions. The app offers a user-friendly interface, allowing students to easily navigate through various subjects and topics, track their progress, and identify areas for improvement.

Dopplegänger

Dopplegänger is a mobile application that allows users to create and share their own digital avatars. The app uses advanced facial recognition technology to generate realistic 3D models based on user selfies, enabling a personalized and immersive experience. Users can customize their avatars with a wide range of features, outfits, and accessories, and use them in various virtual environments, from social media to gaming.
